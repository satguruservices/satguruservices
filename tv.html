TV Services

1. TV Wall Mounting

Secure installation of LED, LCD, or Smart TVs on wall with proper alignment, drilling, and cable management.

2. TV Uninstallation

Safe removal of TV from wall mounts, packing assistance, and socket wire disconnection if needed.

3. Set-Top Box Setup

Installation and connection of DTH or cable set-top box with signal check, HDMI/AV wiring, and basic setup.

4. Smart TV Setup

Connecting Smart TVs to Wi-Fi, app installation (YouTube, Netflix, etc.), account setup, and firmware update.

5. HDMI/AV Cable Setup

Configuration and proper wiring of HDMI or AV cables to speakers, home theatre, or gaming consoles.

6. TV Panel Cleaning

Careful external cleaning of TV screen and back panel using anti-static cleaners and microfiber cloths.

7. TV Sound Issue Check

Inspection of audio output ports, speaker testing, and wiring for fixing low/no sound issues (non-repair).

8. Display Issue Check

Basic troubleshooting of display problems such as no screen, discoloration, or flickering (non-repair).

9. TV Unit Furniture Fixing

Fixing or assembling of TV unit furniture and alignment of mountable racks for display setups.
